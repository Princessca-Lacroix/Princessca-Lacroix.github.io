Matter Background

NovaTech Solutions, Inc., a mid-size SaaS company, is under SEC investigation for alleged securities fraud related to the concealment of a major data breach affecting 2.3 million customers. Simultaneously, the company faces a class action lawsuit from shareholders and a separate whistleblower complaint filed by a former VP of Engineering.

The review covers approximately 100 documents spanning October 2022 – March 2024, including internal emails, executive communications, board meeting minutes, legal hold notices, financial reports, and internal investigation files. Your objective is to identify responsive documents, flag privileged communications, identify hot documents, and apply issue tags for the litigation team.
